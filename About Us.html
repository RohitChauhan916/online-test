About Us

Unique Coaching Classes Started in May 2014 With only 5 Students in a small classroom at Ambedkar Colony, Bijwasan, Since then it went on growing every year. It is ambition & vision of Pankaj Gupta & Jitender Pradhan, to run a class. It has been providing.’ The Complete Guidance For Bright Success’ to 9 to 12 Std Students

The hard work & Sincere efforts of this two man resulted in a huge increase in Students Strength. Soon the need arose to expand.

The main purpose of this Coaching Classes built up a bright academic career to poor & needy Student for their bright Success. Coaching Classes is structured on the principle of consistency and hard work, and be scientific approach based on perfect planning and proper guidance. Unique Classes teaches all Subject under one roof.

The main objective of the top and best known Unique Coaching Classes in Bijwasan , is to provide an edge to Students and Sharpen their Skills, give them a clear picture of what to expect at board level examinations and assist them in their preprations.

The pride of great Success, We are devoting to our all Students.
